The pinnacle of robotics engineering—building machines that move like humans.

## Why Humanoids?

Our world is designed for human bodies. Humanoids can:
- Use stairs, doors, tools designed for humans
- Communicate naturally through gesture and posture
- Work alongside humans intuitively
