Where are humanoid robots actually being used today?

## Current Deployments

### Manufacturing
- **Tesla Optimus** - Assembly line tasks
- **Figure 01** - BMW manufacturing
- **Digit (Agility)** - Amazon warehouses

### Logistics
- Carrying boxes, loading trucks
- Climbing stairs in warehouses
- Last-mile delivery trials

### Healthcare
- Patient lifting assistance
- Rehabilitation support
- Companion robots for elderly

### Research
- Understanding human movement
- Testing AI algorithms
- Developing new capabilities

## The Future

Within 5-10 years, humanoids will likely:
- Work in more factories
- Assist in homes (limited tasks)
- Perform dangerous jobs (inspection, disaster response)
- Become more affordable
